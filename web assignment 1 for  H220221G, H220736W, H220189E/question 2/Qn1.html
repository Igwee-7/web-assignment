Question 1.

You have been approached by a company to create a website for them. Outline the steps you would take to complete this task.

Answer

  • Defining the objectives:- determining the purpose of the website.
  • Choose a Domain Name:- Select a domain name that reflects your company's name, brand, or services. Ensure it is easy to remember, type, and is relevant to your business.
  • Select a Hosting Provider:- Choose a reputable web hosting provider that suits your website's needs (e.g., shared hosting, VPS, dedicated server).
  • Plan Your Website Structure:- Create a site map outlining the main pages and their hierarchy.
  • Content Creation:- Write high-quality, engaging content for your website's pages, including homepage, about us, services/products, contact, and any other relevant sections.
  • Design and Layout:- Choose a website design that aligns with your brand identity and target audience. Create wireframes or mockups to visualize the layout of each page.
  • Develop Your Website:- Choose a website development platform (e.g., WordPress, Joomla, Drupal, or a custom CMS).
  • Testing:- Thoroughly test your website on various browsers and devices to ensure it functions correctly.
  • Launch Your Website:- Point your domain to your hosting server. Remove any "under construction" pages or password protection.
